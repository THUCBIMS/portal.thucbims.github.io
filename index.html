About Us🌍

We are the CBIMS research group from School of software, Tsinghua University. Our research focuses on Building Information Model (BIM), 3D computer vision, and Artificial Intelligence (AI). We aim to bridge the gap between Architecture, Engineering & Construction (AEC) industry and AI.
